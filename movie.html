1) Monster House (2006)

Genre: Animation, Comedy, Family
Duration: 91 minutes
Directed by: Gil Kenan
Actors: Mitchel Musso, Sam Lerner, Spencer Locke
IMDB Score: 6.6/10
Awards: Won all 4 awards and 23 nominations. It was nominated by the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature Film of 2007, and nominated by the Golden Globes as Best Animated Feature Film 2007.

Description :

This movie tells about the lives of suburban children, they see that the neighbor's house is abnormal, so they actively investigate and discover that it is a hiding place of monsters. How will the children fight that spooky house, when the warnings to adults are considered jokes on Halloween.

2) Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark (2019)

Genre: Adventure, Horror, Mystery
Duration: 108 minutes
Directed by: André Ovredal
Actors: Zoe Margaret Colletti, Michael Garza, Gabriel Rush
IMDB Score: 6.2/10
Awards: BMI Cinema Music Award, DGC Craft Award and many more awards and nominations.

Description

The setting of “Scary Stories To Tell In The Dark” takes place in the town of Mill Valley in 1968. Here, the haunting story of the Bellows family is still passed down orally from generation to generation. The origin of these horror stories comes from the suburban mansion where little Sarah Bellows endured a life of suffering and torture by family members.
Sarah publishes a collection of horror stories documenting her own life. But somehow those stories mysteriously came true to the group of young people who discovered the cellar where Sarah Bellows was being held. Will they get away with it?

3) Coraline (2009)

Genre: Animation, Family, Drama
Duration: 100 minutes
Directed by: Henry Selick
Actors: Dakota Fanning, Teri Hatcher, John Hodgman
IMDB Score: 7.7/10
Awards: the 2010 Academy Award for Best Animated Feature Film, nominated by the American Golden Globes as Best Animated Feature Film and other awards.

Description

Coraline is a teenage girl who lives in a large apartment but feels bored and abandoned by her parents. On a rainy night, she finds a special door and accidentally passes through a parallel world. Coraline walked in and was greeted warmly. Everyone called the girl's name correctly (no one had ever called her right before except for the parents and the mice). There she saw another Father and Another Mother, but their eyes were made of buttons, and they did their best to pamper her. When the Other Mother invites Coraline to stay in her world forever, the girl refuses and finds that the alternate reality where she is trapped is just a trick to attract her.

Count Dracula wanted her daughter to stay away from the dangers that the human world brought to her when humans and monsters were still divided, so he built the Transyvania hotel hidden in the deep forest. But suddenly a red-haired guy-Jonathan messed up the peaceful life of father and son.
