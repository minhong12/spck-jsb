5 best entertainment places in Halloween season

Halloween is a ghost festival held on October 31 every year, this is an opportunity for people to freely dress up as their favorite ghost characters. Don't know where to hang out on Halloween? Hanoi has countless places that will help you have a great Halloween night. Let's find out with us right through the article below.

1) Hang Ma

Hang Ma is now in Hang Ma ward, Hoan Kiem district. It is a familiar Halloween place in Hanoi. People come here to shop, take pictures of decorations, clothes for Halloween or simply enjoy the Halloween atmosphere that is hard to find in other places.
Hang Ma Street is a quite famous street in Hanoi and has a long history. Hang Ma Street has long become a familiar destination for people in the capital during the Mid-Autumn Festival. However, you may not know that on Halloween, this street is no less crowded than on Mid-Autumn Festival.
On Halloween, Hang Ma continues to become an ideal place to hang out when a variety of decorations related to the Devil's Festival, from masks, clothes to horror toys are sold here. at a fairly affordable price. However, on special occasions like this, Hang Ma is usually very crowded, so you should visit this place a little early to bring back spooky gifts.
Coming to Hang Ma Street on Halloween, you will enjoy the busy and bustling Halloween atmosphere, you can freely shop for costumes, take pictures and check-in live.

2) Hoan Kiem Lake Walking Street

The Old Quarter area and the pedestrian street of Hoan Kiem Lake area is a place that promises to bring a great Halloween festival atmosphere for young people. With a spacious space and countless street activities held on weekends interspersed with Halloween activities, this place becomes more bustling and unique than ever.
Coming here, you can freely immerse yourself in the crowded people on the pedestrian street around Sword Lake with a unique outfit or makeup without fear of being seen as weird, rather, the more bizarre the better. You can also participate in makeup activities, paint masks and watch street performances.

3) Cinemas in Ha Noi

The cinema is also a great suggestion for parents who are wondering where to let their children go to Halloween. The atmosphere of the Masquerade Festival season is definitely not as bustling as in Hang Ma or the pedestrian street. However, at the cinema, children will have the opportunity to enjoy horror "super products" with all kinds of topics.
What could be more wonderful than when your child experiences the feeling of the hair on the back of his neck, startling his heart with the whole family. If your child does not like to watch horror movies, they can choose Halloween-themed cartoons with light, funny and meaningful plots. These days the cinema is also very crowded. Therefore, parents should actively book tickets early to choose a suitable seat and time frame.

4) Shopping malls

Going to the mall on Masquerade Day is also not a bad idea. At the mall, parents can let their kids go to the movies, shop, play games, eat, etc. Especially on Halloween, these places will be decorated very eye-catchingly to match the atmosphere. festival.
In the center of Hanoi, there is also a place that many people know, especially young people in the capital, that is Royal City Urban Area to participate in the Halloween costume festival. This is the place where young people who love to dress up and cosplay are most eager. It is like a center where young people meet to show off their makeup skills. Parents and children will surely enjoy participating in fun activities here.

5) Cafes

If you do not like shopping or jostling in the crowd, simply want to go out to have fun or meet friends, a coffee shop will be a good suggestion for you.
The rest is to choose a restaurant with the right location and style.
